About AirSafe

AirSafe is a lightweight dashboard designed for college students who train outdoors. It pulls local monitoring station readings from OpenAQ and current weather from OpenWeather.

Data Sources

  • OpenAQ (v3): nearest monitoring location + latest measurements
  • OpenWeather: current conditions for the same coordinates
  • Supabase: saves your favorite locations
