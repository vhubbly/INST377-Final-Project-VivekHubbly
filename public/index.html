Plan outdoor training with confidence.

AirSafe combines local air monitoring (OpenAQ) and current weather (OpenWeather) so students can quickly decide whether to train outside or go indoors.

Open the App Learn More

What you can do

  • Enter coordinates (or use favorites) to check conditions
  • See current weather (temp, humidity, wind)
  • Visualize pollutant readings on a chart

Why it matters

Poor air quality can worsen asthma, allergies, and reduce performance. AirSafe helps students and athletes make safer decisions quickly.
